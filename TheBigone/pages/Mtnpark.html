Mtn Park

The Problem


Mtn Park HOA faced a unique problem as their community had difficulty navigating the website because of all the information and services that were offered. In addition, Mtn Park had three different demographics they were trying to reach.



The Solution


I met with each department head to be able to design a website that is user centric for all three market demographics. I organized all of the information to create buckets of information to easily navigate. The home page is created for residents of the community as well as people who just wanted to join a yoga class for the day. Mtn Park has multiple businesses which I highlight all on the homepage without sacrificing white space.
