Dorey Digital

The Problem


Dorey Design Group is a digital agency which is heavily focuses on development. Dorey is largely built on collaboration with other agencies. Dorey needed a refresh of a new website which would reflect their business model.



The Solution


We changed the name from Dorey Design Group to Dorey Digital. The design for the website is simple. The point is to express they are not a design agency as much as a developer. The design model I created was to show Dorey is not competing with these other creative agencies, but rather Dorey is a future partner. Although the design is simple, I implemented complex front end javascript to contrast the simple layout and to show off their coding skills. The strategy being a website that is done well, but that communicates who Dorey is, and what they can bring to you.
