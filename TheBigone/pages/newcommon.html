Common Projects

White Common Projects might seem unrealistic. We might tell ourselves that it looks nice in pictures, just like a vacation might look in Hawaii. We might tell ourselves that it's a waste of money, a luxury we don't have time for.



In our lives, too often, we don't make room for space. Technology has created a time where we are never bored, and anything that seems like a dull moment reminds us we need to be doing something. However, within space we find time to think; not just update our to-do list. But really give our souls a rest from everything that is trying to fill those spaces. It's more of a discipline than anything else, to give ourselves these luxuries.


Hawaii can seem unrealistic, just as Paris or backpacking around Europe can seem out of our reach. But if we can find space for luxury between our busy schedules, we might be able to find the time; time to walk on the sand in our white Common Projects.
